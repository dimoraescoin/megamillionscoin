Explanation of Allocations

Lottery Prize Pool (15% - 150,000,000 MMC)

A portion of the total token supply will be allocated for the lottery prize pool. This prize pool will be distributed in three separate prize drawings. Each prize is 5% of the circulating supply, but the total prize pool across the three events will amount to 15% of the total supply.

Team & Advisors (10% - 100,000,000 MMC)

This allocation will be for the core team, advisors, and early stakeholders involved in the development and launch of the project. These tokens will be subject to a vesting schedule to ensure long-term commitment.

Staking & Rewards (2% - 20,000,000 MMC)

To incentivize holding MMC, a portion of the total supply will be used to create staking rewards for users who lock their MMC tokens. These rewards could include additional MMC tokens or access to exclusive lottery draws.

Development Fund (5% - 50,000,000 MMC)

This fund is reserved for continued development of the Mega Millions Coin ecosystem, such as building out new features, updates, platform security, and community tools.

Marketing & Partnerships (2.5% - 25,000,000 MMC)

Tokens allocated to marketing campaigns, partnerships, collaborations, and promotional efforts. This includes influencer partnerships, advertising, and other community-building activities.

Liquidity & Exchange Listings (3% - 30,000,000 MMC)

These tokens will be used to ensure liquidity on decentralized exchanges (DEX) and centralized exchanges (CEX). It is also used for initial exchange listings and for ensuring a smooth buying/selling experience for holders.

Community & Airdrops (5% - 50,000,000 MMC)

Airdrops, giveaways, and rewards for the community to drive awareness and participation in the Mega Millions Coin ecosystem. This includes bonuses for early adopters and airdrops to active community members.

Burned / Reserved (70% - 700,000,000 MMC)

A large portion of tokens (70%) will be reserved for future uses or potentially burned over time. This will help to reduce inflation and ensure that the token maintains value as demand increases. The tokens can be burned periodically to reduce the total supply and create deflationary pressure.
